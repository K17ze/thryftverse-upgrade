1. Executive Summary
ThryftVerse is a mobile-first, resale-focused social marketplace built on React Native (Expo) with a Fastify/PostgreSQL backend. This report is the outcome of a full-codebase audit of the frontend ù 115 screens, ~40 shared component families, 27 API service modules, a 5-tab navigation architecture, and a 110-route stack ù cross-referenced against the interaction patterns, information architecture, and business logic of the strongest players in two categories:
Flagship social/visual apps: Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok ù the benchmark for feed engagement, content creation, visual discovery, and retention mechanics.
Marketplace leaders: eBay, Vinted, Depop, Poshmark, Grailed, StockX, Whatnot ù the benchmark for listing flows, trust & safety, buyer/seller protection, pricing intelligence, and monetisation.
1.1 Where ThryftVerse is already strong
The audit found a product that is substantially more ambitious than a typical MVP marketplace. Several capabilities already exceed what Vinted or Depop shipped at comparable maturity:
Fractional co-ownership (ôCo-Ownö) of assets ù an entire trading vertical (asset detail, order book, portfolio, leaderboard, buyout, syndicates) that no mainstream fashion marketplace offers. This is a genuine differentiator closer to StockX-meets-Public.com.
Live auctions with countdowns, sticky bid docks, seller auction centre, and reserve pricing ù comparable in scope to WhatnotÆs core loop (minus live video).
A rich messaging platform ù group chats, bots and a bot builder, message requests, quick replies, voice messages, reactions, reply-quotes, link previews, shared media galleries, archived/muted conversation management. This is beyond what any competitor marketplace ships (Vinted/Depop chat is rudimentary).
Creator Studio ù a layered canvas editor for ôLooksö and ôPostersö with templates, drafts, and a publish pipeline; a foundation for Instagram-style creative content.
Design-system discipline ù design tokens, premium form primitives, flagship component library, skeleton loaders everywhere, haptics, Reanimated/Skia-based animation, i18n string extraction tooling, and CI-style guardrail tests for scroll/animation usage.
Poster stories (ephemeral story-like content), pulse feed, outfit builder, style quiz, visual search entry point, and personalisation surfaces.
