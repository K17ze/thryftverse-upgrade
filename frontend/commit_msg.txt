fix(motion): reduced-motion support + cap list stagger to above-fold items

Per audit section 5.4: "31 of 58 Reanimated screens have no visible
reduced-motion handling" and "Many lists apply index-based
FadeInDown.springify() to every item."

Changes:
- Added useReducedMotion() to 6 remaining screens with entrance
  animations: AboutScreen, ActiveSessionsScreen, HelpSupportScreen,
  PaymentsScreen, PostageScreen, SavedAddressesScreen. All entrance
  animations now return undefined when reduced motion is enabled.
- Added useReducedMotion() to 7 components with entrance animations
  that lacked it: PulseTab (2 cards), LooksTab, EditTab,
  LookPreviewCard, ClosetBoardCard, FlagshipAssetCard,
  FlagshipOrderCard, LookCommentsSheet, RecommendationRail.
- Capped list entrance stagger to above-fold items (max index 6-8)
  across all staggered components. Item 20+ no longer waits a full
  second to appear.
- Removed springify() from list item entrance animations per Design.md
  motion rules ("Press scale 0.97-0.985 with no bounce"). Spring
  bounce on list items causes visual jitter and contradicts the
  snappy-timing motion contract. Replaced with timing-based fades.
- Capped SavedAddressesScreen, OutfitBuilderScreen, TwoFactorSetupScreen
  stagger delays to Math.min(index, 8).
